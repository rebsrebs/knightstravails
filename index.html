Knight's Travails

Find out how many moves it takes a knight to move from one corner of the board to any other square on the chess board. Starting location is 1,1.

In the console, type:

theKnight.knightMoves([x,y])

but for x and y respectively choose a number between 1 and 8 to represent location cooardinates on the chessboard. Read the console for results. Just follow the rules, okay? I haven't done any validation. UI coming ...
